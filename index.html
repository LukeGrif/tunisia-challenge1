Triangle Sum Solver (uses numbers 1..10)

We place six distinct numbers chosen from 1..10 into the triangle (three vertices and three edge middles) so that each side adds up to the same target N. You may optionally exclude multiple numbers from use (e.g. 6 7 9).

Choose a target side sum; commonly valid values are 15–21.
Enter one or more numbers from 1–10, separated by spaces.

Details & Notes

Mapping of positions:

  • v1, v2, v3 = vertices
  • e12 = edge between v1–v2
  • e23 = edge between v2–v3
  • e31 = edge between v3–v1

Side sums:
v1 + e12 + v2, v2 + e23 + v3, v3 + e31 + v1

Logic: iterates all permutations of 6 distinct numbers from 1..10 and keeps those satisfying all three equalities. Multiple excluded numbers can be used.

Tip: try N between 15 and 21 for interesting results.
